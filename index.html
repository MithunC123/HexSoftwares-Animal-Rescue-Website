About Our Mission

Dedicated to creating a world where every animal is treated with compassion and respect

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Paws & Hearts began as a small group of passionate animal lovers determined to make a difference. What started as a local rescue effort has grown into a comprehensive organization serving thousands of animals each year.

We believe every animal deserves a chance at happiness, proper medical care, and a loving home. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to rescue animals from dangerous situations, provide necessary medical treatment, and find them permanent, loving families.

Our Programs

Comprehensive care and support for animals in need

Emergency Rescue

24/7 emergency response team ready to rescue animals from dangerous situations, natural disasters, and abuse cases.

  • Immediate medical attention
  • Safe transportation
  • Crisis intervention

Medical Care

Comprehensive veterinary services including surgeries, vaccinations, and ongoing medical treatment for rescued animals.

  • Surgical procedures
  • Vaccination programs
  • Rehabilitation therapy

Adoption Services

Matching loving families with rescue animals through our comprehensive adoption process and ongoing support.

  • Family matching
  • Home visits
  • Post-adoption support

Education & Outreach

Community education programs promoting responsible pet ownership and animal welfare awareness.

  • School visits
  • Workshop programs
  • Community events
